~ Graphs are the most useful and most used data structure for modeling real life.
~ A graph is a set of values that are related in a pairwise fashion.
~ Graphs have nodes(or vertices) connected by edges.

Exp: It can be anything. Amazon uses them for recommendation engine, Facebook for social network , Google Maps to find shortest node.

?Note : Trees are type of graph.

There are various ways to describe a graph: 
    1. Directional & Undirectional
    2. Weighted(edges having values) & Unweighted 
    3. Cyclic(Closed) & Acyclic(Open)
